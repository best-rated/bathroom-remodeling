Common Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?
A small powder room update might take 2-3 weeks, while a complete master bathroom renovation, including structural changes, could range from 6-10 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after the design phase.
Do I need permits for my remodel?
Yes, permits are typically required for most bathroom remodels involving plumbing, electrical, or structural changes. We handle all necessary permit applications and inspections for you.
What is the average cost?
Costs vary significantly based on size and finishes. A minor refresh could start around $15,000, while a high-end master bath can exceed $60,000. We offer free detailed estimates to give you an accurate price.
Can I live in my home during the remodel?
Yes. We work to contain dust and debris to the bathroom area. However, you will not have access to that specific bathroom during construction, so having a second bathroom is recommended.
